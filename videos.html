Videos

The videos will walk you through using Respond on a day-to-day basis to create sites, update content, and change the look-and-feel of your sites.

Creating a site with Respond

This video walks you through creating a site with Respond CMS.  Creating a site is as easy as adding your site name, typing in an email and password, and selecting a theme.  In under a minute, you will have a beautiful responsive website.

Updating and creating content using the Respond editor

This video will walk you through updating and creating content using the Respond CMS editor.  Updating content is as easy as clicking and typing the new content in the application.  You can add content by dragging and dropping widgets from the top menu bar.

Configuring your theme

This video will walk you through configuring the look-and-feel of your site using the new theme configuration tool in Respond.

Changing your theme

This video will walk you through changing your theme using Respond CMS.  By changing your theme you can alter the entire look-and-feel of your site.  This video also showcases some great themes available in our marketplace.

100% open source, always.

Built by Matthew Smith in Manchester, MO. Respond 6 is an open source, responsive content management system built on the Lumen PHP and Angular 2 frameworks. Respond 6 is built so that you can quickly build beautiful, responsive websites. The latest version features flat-file storage, a beautiful visual editor, and fast static sites.
